[01] The firm

A consultancy that also ships the software.

Most engagements force a choice: hire advisors who hand you a deck, or hire builders who execute someone else's plan. We do both.

vargas.im sits at the table to shape the decision — the architecture, the trade-offs, the path that actually fits your operation — then stays to build, deploy, and run the result. The same people who draw the diagram are accountable for the system in production.

That continuity is the point. No translation loss between strategy and delivery, no finger-pointing between a consultant and an integrator, no proof-of-concept that quietly dies before launch. One accountable partner across the whole lifecycle.
